Help

MyChoiceMaker presents a simple interface for creating and viewing Choice Boards for children with communication Challenges. Choice Boards can be created with a picture/word combination.

Create New Board:

A new choice board can be added by clicking on the '+' button on main page. Boards can be created for horizontal or vertical scanning with up to three choices, as per the visual preference of the child; or in a grid arrangement of upto 6 choices. Choice items can be added by clicking the choice frame and saving a picture/text to it. Audio can be recorded for the choice item by clicking on the recording button. Boards can be saved for later viewing. Saved boards are displayed on the main page.

Delete A Board:

An existing board can be deleted by dragging and dropping the board to the Delete button on the main page.

View Board:

A previously saved board can be viewed by clicking on its image on the main page. In the view mode, the selected choice item is momentarily highlighted for visual support. If the choice item has an audio recording, the audio plays on selection.
